Juniper

Push notifications across your devices

Juniper is a cross-platform application that listens for special tweets that you and your close friends send, to push notifications to your devices and control them natively in cool ways. Right now, Android, iOS, Windows, OSX, and the web are supported, with Linux on the way!

For more information or to manage your account, please go to http://juniperapp.com.

Special thanks to Titanium Desktop for the incredible cross-platform development framework and Mihaiciuc Bogdan for the beautiful icons.
